# Silvet

Silvet is a veterinary cardiology clinic. The website serves as the public-facing homepage for the practice, primarily targeting Polish-speaking pet owners with alternative language support.

## Language

**Silvet**:
The brand name of the veterinary cardiology practice.
_Avoid_: SilVet, Sil-Vet

**Locale**:
One of the four supported languages on the website: Polish (pl), English (en), German (de), or Portuguese (pt). Polish is the primary locale.
_Avoid_: Language, translation

**Clinic**:
The physical veterinary cardiology practice location. Referred to in the Footer contact section with address, phone, and email.
_Avoid_: Office, surgery

**Echocardiography**:
Cardiac ultrasound imaging service for dogs and cats.
_Avoid_: Echo, heart scan

**Cardiac Screening**:
Pre-anesthetic and breed-specific heart evaluations.
_Avoid_: Heart check, cardiac check-up

**Heart Disease Management**:
Ongoing treatment plans for chronic cardiac conditions in animals.
_Avoid_: Treatment, cardiac therapy

**Emergency Cardiology**:
Urgent assessment and treatment of acute cardiac events in animals.
_Avoid_: Heart emergency, cardiac emergency

**Appointment**:
A scheduled visit to the clinic. The primary call-to-action on the website.
_Avoid_: Booking, reservation, consultation

**Client**:
The pet owner who engages with the clinic. The primary user of the website.
_Avoid_: Customer, patient (reserved for the animal)

**Pet**:
The animal patient receiving veterinary care.
_Avoid_: Patient (acceptable in clinical context but Pet is preferred), animal

**Service**:
A specific medical offering listed in the Services grid (echocardiography, cardiac screening, heart disease management, emergency cardiology).
_Avoid_: Product, offering

**Home Visit**:
A proposed service type where the veterinarian visits the client's home. Planned for future phases.
_Avoid_: House call, at-home visit

**Staff**:
All personnel at the clinic: doctors, technicians, administrative staff. Used in CMS context for team profiles.
_Avoid_: Team, employees, people

**Doctor**:
The veterinarian (specialist cardiologist). Distinguished from Technician in the staff hierarchy.
_Avoid_: Vet, specialist (when referring to a person)

**Technician**:
Veterinary technical staff who assist with diagnostics and patient care.
_Avoid_: Assistant, nurse

**No-Show**:
A missed appointment without prior cancellation. Relevant to the booking and communication system.
_Avoid_: Missed appointment, absence

**Schedule**:
The clinic's operating hours and individual staff availability. Used for appointment booking.
_Avoid_: Calendar, timetable, roster

**CMS**:
Content Management System for the dashboard — used to manage staff profiles, services, working hours, and communication templates.
_Avoid_: Admin panel, backend, control panel

**Communication Template**:
Pre-written message templates (email/SMS) for appointment confirmations, reminders, and follow-ups. Locale-specific.
_Avoid_: Message template, email template, notification template

**Verification**:
The process of confirming that a client's contact information (phone, email) is valid before enabling appointment booking.
_Avoid_: Validation, confirmation

**Analytics**:
Clinic-level usage statistics: appointment volume, no-show rate, popular services, locale distribution. Future feature.
_Avoid_: Reports, stats, insights
